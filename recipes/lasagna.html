Lasagna

Description

This is the most delicious piece of lasagna you will ever eat. The ingredients and steps to cook this will leave you wanting more.

Ingredients

  • Vegan Cheese
  • Pasta
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. Boil pasta
  2. Put boiled pasta in dish
  3. Bake in the oven until burnt
  4. Serve cold
